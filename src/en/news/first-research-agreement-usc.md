Fundación Martina has signed its first scientific collaboration agreement with the
**School of Medicine at the Universidade de Santiago de Compostela (USC)**, to launch a
line of research into the genetic and molecular basis of ataxias associated with the
VPS13D gene.

The agreement will fund translational research aimed at improving our understanding of
the molecular mechanisms behind this family of ataxias, with the goal of laying the
groundwork for future diagnostic and treatment approaches.

This is the first scientific agreement signed by the Foundation since its founding, and
we hope it marks the beginning of a network of collaborations with research centres
across Galicia, Spain and internationally.
